About Founder

Alec Soth is an American photographer, based in Minneapolish. Soth makes "large-scale American projects" featuring the mid-western US. New York Times art critic Hilarie M. Sheets wrote that he has made a "photographic career out of finding chemistry with strangers" and photographs "loners and dreamers".
